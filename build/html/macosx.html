Keyboard Shortcuts¶

Keyboard symbols:

Description Symbol Unicode
Shift ⇧ U+21e7
Shift lock ⇪ U+21ea
Command ⌘ U+2318
ESC ⎋ U+238b
Eject ⏏ U+23cf
Option ⎇ U+2387
Control ⌃ U+2303
Space ␣ U+2423
Power ⌽ U+233D

Note

To insert one of the above symbols:

  1. Copy and paste from the Symbol column
  2. ⌃ ⌘ ␣
Shortcut(s) Description
⌽ Press to turn on your Mac or wake your Mac from sleep. Press and hold for 1.5 seconds while your Mac is awake to display a dialog asking if you want to sleep, restart, or shut down.If you don’t want to wait 1.5 seconds, press ⌃ ⌽ or ⌃ ⏏. Press and hold for 5 seconds to force your Mac to turn off.
⌃ ⌘ ⌽ Force your Mac to restart.
⌃ ⇧ ⏏ Put your displays to sleep. Also ⌃ ⇧ ⎋
⌃ ⌘ ⏏ Quit all apps, then restart your Mac. If any open documents have unsaved changes, you’ll be asked whether you want to save them.
⌃ ⌘ ⎋ Quit all apps, then shut down your Mac. If any open documents have unsaved changes, you’ll be asked whether you want to save them. Also ⌃ ⎇ ⌘ ⏏
⇧ ⌘ Q Log out of your macOS user account. You’ll be asked to confirm.
⌃ ⇧ ⌘ Q Log out of your macOS user account immediately, w/o being asked to confirm.
